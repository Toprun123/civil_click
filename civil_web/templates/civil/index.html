Civil_Click is a simple single player game that works by clicking to gain basic resources and eventually branching out to build your own factories.

The goal is to collect as many stars as you can (use our helpful tooltips to figure out how to do it).

Each star is ten times harder to gain than the previous one, let's see how far you can go.

This game will also save your game state to your account automatically every 10 seconds, so you dont lose any progress.

You can also sign in to your account anywhere to continue playing from your initial game state.

It is also impossible to lose your stars once you gain it.

Enjoy playing!
